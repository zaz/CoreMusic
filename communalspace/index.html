Communal Space

Core Music always has a friendly atmosphere and the ethos of ‘a community shop where anyone can try instruments and ask for honest advice regardless of age, ability or style’ can be felt throughout the premises.


The communal space is an area where people can meet in the shop to enjoy fresh coffee and other refreshments, check our noticeboards and find out what is happening at Core Music!


Some clubs such as the Acoustic Sessions also use this space for small casual jams and to talk techniques and inspirations. It also makes for a laid back area to relax before lessons, workshop and exams.
