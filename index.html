Welcome to Core Music!

A little bit about us

Opened in 2008, Core Music is a music shop and community music facility located in Hexham, Northumberland.

We sell a variety of products in-store including guitars, ukuleles, drums, amps and music books. Gift vouchers, accessories, equipment, music gifts are always available in-store as well as fresh coffee and other refreshments in our communal area.

Core Music is also a key access point to community music in Northumberland. Our aim is to benefit the community by providing access to music for anyone, no matter their circumstances, age or ability.
We believe that music should be for everyone, and we’re working hard to make that a reality.
