About Us

A BIT MORE ABOUT US

Core Music is a key access point to community music in Northumberland. Our aim is to benefit the community by providing access to music for anyone, no matter their circumstances, age or ability.


We believe that music should be for everyone, and we’re working really hard to make that a reality. We organise events such as concerts and seminars, provide a workspace for local tutors and run workshops on a variety of topics. Many clubs, groups and bands also make use of our spacious premises as a venue for rehearsal and meeting space.


Core Music is a social enterprise and a not for profit organisation, which means that any money we do make goes back into the company to continue to benefit the local community. We are led by a board of directors who collectively have a vast amount of knowledge concerning making and recording music, as well as building and sustaining community organisations and event management.



All the money taken in through the shop and activities is used for:

  • Employing local people.
  • Offering volunteering opportunities.
  • Subsidising music clubs and sessions.
  • Supporting community music events.
  • Providing music-related advice for individuals and organisations.
  • Providing local music teachers with a base to work from.

Please shop local, support us with your custom and encourage your friends and colleagues to support your social enterprise.


FRIENDS OF CORE MUSIC

Over the past 5 years, Core Music has provided a valued range of facilities and services for people of all ages who enjoy music in Hexham, Tynedale and beyond. To ensure the continued success of this outstanding community resource we have launched the 'Friends of Core Music'.


You can find out more about the Friends of Core Music by reading the full release letter here: Friends of Core Music - Letter.
