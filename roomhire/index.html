Room Hire

We have 4 rooms available to hire for rehearsals, meetings, interviews and training. We have 2 large rooms (one with level ground floor access) capable of accommodating up to 20 to 25 people, and 3 smaller rooms capable of holding up to 8.


All have plenty of electrical sockets and can be supplied with a variety of equipment such as amplifiers, pa, music stands, cd players, flip charts, projector, tables, chairs and so on.


Prices start from £6.60 per hour. Contact us for more info and check out the gallery below to see some images of the rooms.
